My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to user research revolves around empathy, curiosity, and methodical inquiry. I prioritize understanding users' needs, motivations, and pain points to inform design decisions effectively.

User Empathy

In my UX design process, I prioritize user empathy by immersing myself in their experiences, actively listening, and observing to deeply understand their perspectives and needs.

Qualitative Interview

In my UX design practice, I emphasize the value of qualitative interviews as a means to delve into users' thoughts and experiences. Through open-ended questioning and active listening, I uncover rich insights that inform user-centric design decisions, ultimately enhancing the overall user experience.

My Work

MasoniVerse

MasoniVerse is a comprehensive gateway to all essential GMU resources and services, conveniently accessible from one central webpage. From academic tools like BlackBoard and official Mason resources such as Billing and Mason360 – MasoniVerse consolidates everything a GMU student needs in one customizable user-friendly interface.

Barter Buddies

Barter Buddies is a trading platform for individuals seeking to exchange goods and services without using traditional currency. The app’s innovative platform simplifies the trading process with a user-friendly interface with features for listing and browsing, and ensures trust through robust verification systems for a community of users who value sustainability.

PawPrint Partners

PawPrint Partners aims to solve the challenges both new and veteran pet owners face in connecting with others, adopting animals, as well as finding our lost pets. The current process is often stressful and inefficient. The goal was to create an app to create a supportive and compassionate pet-loving community.
